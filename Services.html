FINANCIAL AID QUERIES

Financial Aid

BookMySlot streamlines the financial assistance process by allowing students to schedule dedicated appointments for all funding-related needs. Whether you need help with tuition payments, financial clearance, NSFAS funding, bursaries, or account queries, the platform ensures you receive assistance at a specific time without waiting in long queues.

By introducing structured time slots, students are guided on when and where to go, reducing confusion, overcrowding, and unnecessary delays. This ensures faster, more organized, and efficient service delivery.

Challenges Students Face

  • Long queues during registration and clearance periods
  • Inability to register due to outstanding fees
  • Delays in NSFAS or bursary funding approvals
  • Missing lectures while waiting for assistance
  • High stress and uncertainty about financial status

ACCOMODATION QUERIES

Accommodation

BookMySlot allows students to conveniently schedule appointments for all accommodation-related services, including residence applications, room allocations, maintenance requests, and housing queries. Instead of standing in long queues, students can book a time slot and receive assistance efficiently.

The system helps reduce overcrowding in housing offices while ensuring that students are assisted in a timely and organized manner without disrupting their academic schedules.

Challenges Students Face

  • Not being allocated residence rooms on time
  • Lack of temporary accommodation at the start of the year
  • Students forced to travel long distances or return home
  • Delays in application processing and approvals
  • Overcrowding in residence offices during peak periods

SUPPORT

Support Services (SRC)

The Student Representative Council (SRC) plays a vital role in supporting students and ensuring their concerns are heard. Through BookMySlot, students can book sessions with SRC representatives to receive guidance, advocacy, and assistance with complex issues.

Whether you are facing challenges with financial aid, academic exclusion, accommodation, or general student life, the SRC is available to help escalate matters and find solutions.

What the SRC Provides

  • Representation in discussions with university management
  • Help with exclusions, appeals, and registration issues
  • Assistance with NSFAS and bursary challenges
  • Access to student resources and emergency assistance

Challenges Students Face

  • Academic exclusion or inability to register
  • Accommodation and housing crises
  • Unresolved financial aid issues
  • Missing opportunities like Work Integrated Learning (WIL)
  • Being forced to return home due to unresolved issues
