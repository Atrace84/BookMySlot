Welcome To BookMySlot

BookMySlot is designed to eliminate long queues and improve the student experience through a smart booking system.

The Problem

Students spend hours in queues, missing lectures and experiencing unnecessary stress.

Our Solution

An online system that allows students to book time slots instead of waiting in line.

Our Goal

To create a more efficient, organized, and stress-free campus experience.

OUR SERVICES

We Offer BOOKING SERVICES For

Financial Aid

BookMySlot streamlines the financial assistance process by allowing students to book scheduled appointments for all funding-related needs. Whether you have questions about tuition payments, financial clearance, scholarships, or other funding matters, you can now receive support without standing in long lines. By providing exact time slots and guidance on where to go, the system reduces stress, prevents missed lectures, and ensures that students get timely, organized, and efficient assistance.

Accommodation

Our platform allows students to conveniently schedule appointments for accommodation support, including applications, room changes, and housing queries. This reduces overcrowding and ensures timely assistance without disrupting academic activities.

Support

"Need a voice in your corner? The UMP Student Representative Council (SRC) is here to serve and support you. Whether you are facing challenges with NSFAS funding, academic exclusions, campus accommodation, or general student life, our representatives are ready to intervene.

Book a session with the SRC to receive:

Advocacy: Representation in discussions with university management. Academic & Financial Guidance: Assistance with registration hurdles and bursary enquiries. Welfare Support: Access to student-led funds and resources for essential needs. Don't navigate your university journey alone—reach out and let your voice be heard."
