Welcome to the Help Center

How do I book a service?
Go to Home → Book Now → Select service → Choose time.
How do I cancel my booking?
Go to Booking page and click 'Cancel Existing Booking'.
How do I know my booking was cancelled?
A confirmation email will be sent to you.
What happens if I am late for my appointment?
Your appointment is automatically cancelled.
What if I cancelled my booking by mistake?
You will have to book for another available slot because when you cancel our system allows for everyone to book that reopened slot.
How long will my meeting last?
Each session lasts for only 10 minutes.
How many students do they take per day?
Only 48 students are assisted per day.
When do they operate and for how many hours?
Mondays to Fridays, 08:00–12:00 and 13:00–17:00.
